Welcome to Young @ Heart Balloons!

Young at Heart is your ultimate creative professional events provider and pioneer in balloon art works in Northern, Mindanao since 1998. We specialize in trendy balloon works, birthday packages, wedding packages and party accessories.

Young at Heart Balloons was created through the collaborative efforts of three dynamic sisters led by Helen Grace Maestrado, Marites Maestrado Miranda and Raquel Maestrado Tabbay. These ladies, all with undeniably vibrant personalities, have the experience and creativity to make wonderful weddings, parties, any events for their clients.

Your ultimate, creative professional event provider and pioneer in balloon art works in NORTHERN MINDANAO,PHILIPPINES since 1998.

Our main problem is the shortage of stockage. To avoid this problem, we accept advance orders to provide our customers with better and quality service.
